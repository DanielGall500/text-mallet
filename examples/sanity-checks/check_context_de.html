Leipzig is the most populous city in the German state of Saxony . The city has a population of 633 , 592 residents as of 31 December 2025 . It is the eighth - largest city in Germany and is part of the Central German Metropolitan Region . Leipzig is located about 150 km ( 90 mi ) southwest of Berlin , in the southernmost part of the North German Plain ( the Leipzig Bay ) , at the confluence of the White Elster and its tributaries Pleiße and Parthe .
